HappyFeet Activity Club - Website Structure & Details

=========================================
1. GLOBAL ELEMENTS & APP STRUCTURE
=========================================
- App.jsx: The main entry point. It handles all the routing for the application, lazy-loads the pages for better performance (code splitting), manages the global ScrollToTop behavior when navigating between pages, and controls the initial SplashScreen and the RegisterPopup.
- SplashScreen.jsx: A playful, engaging loading screen that appears when the user first visits the website, giving the React application time to load behind the scenes.
- RegisterPopup.jsx: A global modal that automatically prompts users to register/sign up for a newsletter or updates if they haven't done so. It appears after a short delay on the site.
- FloatingNavbar.jsx: The persistent navigation menu that floats at the bottom/top of the screen. It allows users to quickly jump between all the main pages (Home, Book Event, Gallery, Testimonials, Kits, Games, Activity, About Us, Contact Us).
- Footer.jsx: The global footer displayed at the bottom of pages, containing company information, quick links, contact details, and social media icons.
- FloatingConfetti.jsx: A background visual effect component that renders playful floating shapes/confetti to match the brand's aesthetic.

=========================================
2. PAGES & THEIR FUNCTIONS
=========================================

--- A. LANDING PAGE (/) ---
File: src/pages/Landing.jsx
Function: The main showcase page designed to capture attention and direct users to key services.
Components Used:
- HeroSlider: A dynamic, auto-playing image slider at the top of the page with call-to-action buttons.
- EnvelopeQuote: A visually striking section designed to look like a welcome card or envelope containing the HappyFeet mission quote.
- ImageGallery: A "Captured Moments" photo grid displaying a quick glimpse of past events.
- CategoriesSpread: A visual layout showcasing the different types of events HappyFeet organizes (e.g., Birthdays, Corporate, Carnivals).
- DecorThemes: A horizontally scrolling carousel displaying various decoration themes available for parties.
- ClientTestimonials: A section showcasing reviews and ratings from past clients.
- EventTimeline: A visual step-by-step timeline explaining the process of how HappyFeet plans an event from start to finish.

--- B. KITS PAGE (/kits) ---
File: src/pages/Kits.jsx
Function: Showcases the physical DIY activity kits that HappyFeet sells for kids to do at home or at parties.
Components Used:
- KitsHeroSection: The introductory header for the kits page.
- KitsSection & KitCard: Displays a grid of available kits with details, images, and pricing/booking information.

--- C. BOOK EVENT PAGE (/book-event) ---
File: src/pages/BookEvent.jsx
Function: The primary conversion page where users can fill out a form with their event details (date, type of event, number of kids, etc.) to request a booking or quotation from HappyFeet.

--- D. GALLERY PAGES ---
The gallery is split into multiple specialized pages to organize a large amount of media.
1. Main Gallery (/gallery)
   File: src/pages/Gallery.jsx
   Function: The entry point to the photo and video gallery, allowing users to browse by categories or specific events.
2. Event Details (/gallery/event/:id)
   File: src/pages/EventDetails.jsx
   Function: A dynamic page that loads the specific photo album and details for a single, past event.
3. Category Media (/gallery/category/:slug)
   File: src/pages/CategoryMedia.jsx
   Function: Displays all photos and videos related to a specific category (e.g., all "Birthday" media).
4. All Media (/gallery/all-media)
   File: src/pages/AllMedia.jsx
   Function: A comprehensive, filterable grid of all media assets across the entire company history.

--- E. DECOR THEMES DETAILS (/decor-themes/:slug) ---
File: src/pages/DecorThemeDetails.jsx
Function: When a user clicks on a theme (like "Jungle Safari" or "Space") from the Landing page, they are taken here to see a detailed description, color palettes, and photos specifically for that theme.

--- F. TESTIMONIALS PAGE (/testimonial) ---
File: src/pages/Testimonials.jsx
Function: A dedicated page for social proof, showing a comprehensive list of client reviews, video testimonials, and success stories.

--- G. GAMES PAGE (/games) ---
File: src/pages/Games.jsx
Function: Showcases the interactive games and physical setups HappyFeet provides for parties. 
Components Used:
- GamesSection / GameCard: A grid of available games.
- GameModal: A popup that shows more details or rules about a specific game when clicked.

--- H. ACTIVITY PAGE (/activity) ---
File: src/pages/Activity.jsx
Function: Details the specific craft, art, and play activities offered by HappyFeet.
Components Used:
- ActivityHero: Header section.
- ActivityCards & ActivityCarousel: Visual displays of the different activities kids can participate in.

--- I. ABOUT & CONTACT PAGE (/about, /contact) ---
File: src/pages/AboutContact.jsx
Function: A combined page that tells the company's story and provides contact methods.
Components Used:
- OurStory: Text and images detailing how HappyFeet started.
- FounderSection: Information about the founders/team.
- ContactSection & ContactComicForm: A stylized, playful contact form for users to send general inquiries, along with a map and contact details.

=========================================
3. DATA MANAGEMENT (Currently Local, Moving to Sanity CMS)
=========================================
The website currently relies on static local JavaScript files (like pastEvents.js, categories.js) to populate the pages with data. The architecture is currently being transitioned to Sanity CMS (configured in the `studio/` folder) which will allow the client to update text, images, themes, and gallery items dynamically without touching the code.
